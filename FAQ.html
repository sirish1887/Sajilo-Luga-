Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sajilo Luga?
Sajilo Luga is a modern clothing brand that provides stylish, comfortable, and affordable apparel for all age groups.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes! We ship worldwide with reliable courier services. Delivery times may vary depending on the destination.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you’ll receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use it to track your package anytime.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and Cash on Delivery (COD) in select regions.
Can I return or exchange my order?
Absolutely. You can request a return or exchange within 7 days of delivery, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging.
